Objectives

  • Identify, nurture and enhance the potential in every DTES Happiest Mind for productivity, people happiness and achievement levels.
  • Enable each DTES Happiest Mind with - a guide for career path, technical and related topics.
  • Enhance the professional network of people within the BU and provide high quality interaction opportunities; Increased people ownership across the BU – “People happiness is everyone’s business”
  • Provide leadership growth opportunities for senior Happiest Minds by providing mentoring to other Happiest Minds
  • Build a solid platform for rapidly resolving queries, issues and providing directions to each individual, with clear go-to person identification & process.
